## 1.1 What You Are Working With

In this networking lab, students work with **pods**.

A **pod** is a **self-contained practice network** designed to simulate a real-world enterprise environment. Each pod includes multiple networking devices:

- 4 Cisco 1921 Routers
- 3 Cisco 2801
- 3 Cisco Catalyst 3560 PoE-48 Switches
- 4 Cisco ASA (Adaptive Security Appliance) 5505 Series

You will need to turn on the pods, plug in cross-over ethernet cables into each router, switch, and ASA you wish to configure. There are multiple other connection too such as serial ports.

> Think of a pod as a mini data center networking environment that you can access from a terminal.
